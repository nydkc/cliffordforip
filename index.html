Introduction

Greetings Key Clubbers,

Welcome to my campaign website! My name is Clifford Young and I am honored to be seeking the office of Key Club International President!

Last year, you all graciously elected me to the office of Trustee. Since then, I've been involved in the great milestones of our organization. Throughout the year, I have had the privilege of seeing how all of our different members interpret service. Additionally, I was able to learn more about what makes our organization the best service organization in the world.

Just as Key Club spans the globe, I too hail from overseas. Although, I am from the New York District my family immigrated to the United States about 30 years ago from the islands of Jamaica and Saint Vincent. (I do serve the Jamaica District also!)

Aside from Key Club, I pursue cycling through a club at my school, I have been playing the piano for the past eight years, and I love to learn about different cultures both at home and worldwide. I can't wait to meet each of you at ICON; Let's make convention a week to remember!

#CliffordForIP

FAQ

What do you hope to accomplish for Key Club International?

Key Club International has achieved record breaking membership, expanded to new countries, raised millions of dollars, and served the community in ways that are unimaginable. As we embark on our 92nd year, we need a leader who can build upon our accomplishments and set precedents for the future. My plan of enlightening, elevating, and energizing Key Club and its members will help us grow stronger.
